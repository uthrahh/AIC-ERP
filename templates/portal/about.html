Vision

To become India's premier catalyst for smart and clean mobility innovation, empowering entrepreneurs to build sustainable, future-ready smart solutions that transform industries, uplift communities and drive global competitiveness.

Mission

To nurture and accelerate high-potential startups in the smart and clean mobility sector by providing world-class incubation support, cutting-edge research access and technology, strategic mentorship, industry partnerships and go-to-market opportunities empowering innovators to drive India's transition toward a smart, sustainable and connected mobility ecosystem.
